phishing

For this project, we were assigned to create a website to raise awareness about a social issue and advocate for change. We were able to choose any topic, and I decided to go with Phishing. My website consisted of two pages, one page dedicated to giving information about the topic of phishing and another page dedicated to giving individual tips and strategies to fight against phishing.

reflection
